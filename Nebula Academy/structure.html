What is DeFi?

DeFi stands for Decentralized Finance. Decentralized finance is one of the many applications of blockchain technology. It enables access to traditional financial services in a much faster, simpler, more efficient, and user-friendly manner. As investors, it allows us to generate passive income, borrow or lend money, and make investments, all without centralized intermediaries and within minutes. Everyone has access to significant opportunities without any bureaucratic procedures and without seeking permission from anyone, thanks to decentralization. Welcome to the financial revolution!

How to use DeFi?

To access decentralized finance, it is necessary to install a decentralized wallet. One of the most well-known wallets is Metamask. When installing it, you will need to create an 'account on the blockchain.' (Don't worry, it's simple and will take less than 5 minutes). To create an account on the blockchain, you will need to write down 12 words on a piece of paper. These 12 words are the Seed Phrase or private key that will allow you to control your cryptocurrencies. You should not share this seed phrase with anyone as it could lead to the theft of your funds. Write down the words on paper and keep it safe, don't lose it. In addition to these 12 words, you will need to create a password that will grant access to your Metamask account on the device where it is installed. This password will not work if you try to access the account from another device. If you lose or forget the password, don't worry because you can still access it using the seed phrase or private key. Let's Install Metamask: Please click on this link (https://metamask.io/) , it will open the official Metamask website where you can install the wallet. Follow the steps provided and then return to this window to continue with the course. If you have already installed Metamask, congratulations! You now have your account on the blockchain. With that wallet, you can connect to dApps (decentralized applications) to access their services. How do you connect Metamask to a dApp? Very simple, just click on this button: Now you are connected to this dApp. Up there, you can see your public key or public address on the blockchain. With this public address, you will be able to receive funds and see the amount you have. The public address will also appear at the top of your Metamask extension. You can create multiple blockchain accounts using a single private key. To do this, open the Metamask extension and click on the circle icon in the upper right corner, which represents your accounts. From there, simply click on 'create account' and assign a unique name to your newly created account.
